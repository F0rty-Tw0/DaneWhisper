Dane Whisper

Dane Whisper is Aleksey Blinov from Lodeynoe Pole, Russia.

His music sneaks up to the depths of the soul due to its penetration. Many compositions are made to listen in a hard moment of your life. And some are written to listen to the live events. His tracks have a pleasant sound, quite unlike other artists, directions of synthwave supporting the sound of 80s music. He is also the owner of "Northern Synth" label, which allows him to make musical releases directly. Now, this label counts up to 13 tracks from various artists and increasing each day.
